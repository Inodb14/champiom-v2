About

A Builder.
A Storyteller.
A Force.

Born in Lagos, raised between London and San Francisco, ahimachal Champion has spent nearly two decades disrupting the intersection of technology, finance, and culture.


His portfolio spans three successful exits, three continents, and over $1.2 billion in combined enterprise value created. His philosophy is simple: "Systems beat intentions. Build the system."
